About Us

We are a team of lighting designers which get their experience in the lighting field from the last 22 years. At the beginning we started through a wholesales company by making lighting solutions for different projects (residential, urban, corporate, industrial, social, cultural and commercial).

Now, in the last 13 years we provide professional lighting design consultancy that offers a comprehensive range of consultancy services designed to support the lighting industry, but not just that, we can offer production of a new range of luminaire for complete lighting solutions through our partner's facilities.

Exelerion draws together excellence and experience in lighting design and technology, with associated technical calculations, architectural feeling, and computer simulated imagery.
